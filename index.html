Preventing Frozen Pipes

Frozen pipes are common during prolonged cold snaps. To prevent freezing, insulate exposed pipework with foam sleeves, keep kitchen and bathroom cabinet doors open to allow warm air circulation, and maintain a minimum home temperature of around 12°C. Letting a slow trickle of water run during severe frosts can also stop pressure build-up that causes bursts. If freezing does occur, never use open flames to thaw pipes; contact a professional for safe thawing and repair.

Keeping Drains Clear

Regular prevention is far cheaper than emergency clearing. Install strainers in sinks and showers, avoid pouring cooking fats down drains and dispose of coffee grounds in the bin. For slow-draining sinks use enzyme-based cleaners monthly—avoid harsh chemicals that damage pipes. If you notice recurring blockages, we offer CCTV inspection and targeted cleaning to remove roots, grease and debris and recommend long-term maintenance plans.

DIY vs Professional

Small tasks like changing a washer or replacing a simple trap are reasonable for a confident DIYer. Anything involving gas, mains water, structural changes or complex pipework should be left to Gas Safe and qualified plumbers. Incorrect repairs can lead to leaks, code breaches and insurance problems—when in doubt, request a professional survey to save time and cost later.

Boiler Efficiency

Regular servicing keeps boilers running safely and efficiently. Upgrading to a modern condensing boiler and adding controls such as thermostatic radiator valves or a smart thermostat can cut fuel bills. We provide energy audits, recommend the right boiler size for your property and carry out compliant installations including system flushing and commissioning.

Bleeding Radiators

Bleeding radiators removes trapped air preventing even heating. With the heating off, open the bleed valve with a key until water appears, then close. If radiators still underperform we can powerflush the system, balance radiators and replace faulty valves to restore full efficiency across your home.

Smart Thermostats

Smart thermostats let you control heating remotely, set schedules and reduce wasted energy. They can integrate with boilers and zoned controls to prioritise comfort where you need it. We install, configure and test units from major brands and ensure compatibility with your current heating system.

What is an Emergency?

An emergency is an issue that risks property damage, health or safety—examples include uncontrolled leaks, gas smells, loss of heating in freezing weather, and sewage backup. For these call our 24/7 number immediately. For non-urgent problems you can book a scheduled appointment during normal working hours.

Handling a Burst Pipe

If a pipe bursts, shut off your mains stopcock, turn off electrics near the water and move valuables away. Contain the flow with buckets and towels and call emergency plumbing. We provide temporary shutdowns, pipe replacement and water-damage mitigation, and work with insurers when needed.

Severe Blockages

Persistent slow drains and foul odours can indicate serious blockages or root ingress. We use CCTV surveys to pinpoint the location of blockages and high-pressure jetting to clear pipes without excavation where possible. After clearing, we advise on steps to prevent recurrence, such as grease traps or drain covers.

Planning a Bathroom

Successful bathroom projects start with planning pipe routes, ventilation and waterproofing. We help select fixtures, design accessible layouts, and coordinate trades for tiling and electrics. Our project-managed installations deliver compliant, attractive bathrooms on time and on budget.

Wet Rooms Explained

Wet rooms require a fully waterproofed floor and carefully designed falls to the drain. We assess the subfloor and structure, design appropriate drainage, and install membrane systems that meet building regulations—delivering a seamless, low-maintenance bathing space.

Kitchen Plumbing

Kitchen plumbing for appliances like dishwashers and fridges needs correct isolation valves, secure waste connections and often a dedicated feed. Incorrect installation can cause leaks or void warranties. We install appliance drains, test for leaks and tidy pipework to leave your kitchen neat and fully functional.
